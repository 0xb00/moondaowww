## Minion FAQ

<h4>
  <details>
    <summary>Q: What is a Minion? </summary>
  
    <p>&nbsp;</p>
  
    A: Minions enable you DAO to call arbitrary contracts, enabling you to do many things such as manage ENS, collect NFTs, manage treasury in DeFi, etc.
  
  </details>
</h4>

<h4>
  <details>
    <summary>Q: Can a Minion hold NFTs? </summary>
  
    <p>&nbsp;</p>
  
    A: Yes, they can.
  
  </details>
</h4>

<h4>
  <details>
    <summary>Q: Does a Minion address need to be verified to interact with other contracts? </summary>
  
    <p>&nbsp;</p>
  
    A: No, the minion does not need to be verified, as long as the contract address that the minion is interacting with is verified. 
  
  </details>
</h4>

<h4>
  <details>
    <summary>Q: I'm summoning a Minion. What does "Quorum" refer to? </summary>
  
    <p>&nbsp;</p>
  
    A: Quorum refers to the percentage of total voing power required to pass the Minion proposal 
  
  </details>
</h4>
