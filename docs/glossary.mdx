## Glossary of common terms

**Internal Balance**
Funds received from the DAO to your member address. Withdraw to your wallet from your profile page in the DAO.

**GuildKick**
Converts any Shares into Loot, to remove voting power.

**Loot**
Grants proportionate ownership of core treasury, without the voting rights.

**Ragequit**
Burn some amount of shares and/or loot in exchange for proportionate amount of tokens in the DAO. One doesn't have to burn all shares, and can ragequit any amount at anytime.

**Shares**
Grants voting rights and proportionate ownership of core treasury.

**Summon**
Launch a new DAO

**Summoner**
The original member or members who summoned the DAO
