## How to Summon a DAO

Choose your network and [Summon a DAO](https://app.daohaus.club/summon) with one click. Along with Ethereum Mainnet, you can also summon a dao on these networks:

- [xDAI](/networks#xdai)
- [Polygon (formerly Matic)](/networks#polygon)
- [IDChain (by BrightID)](/networks#idchain)

### Easy Mode

Choose a template that is right for you and Summon away.

#### Templates

**Guilds**

**Grants**

**Investments**

**Projects/Protocols**

**Social Clubs**

### Hard Mode

For Advanced users only, who know what they are doing. If you really need to do something custom, find us in our Discord and we'll help you out.
